About Niche Clean Energy

Niche Clean Energy is an Indian company that develops renewable energy projects, with a focus on large-scale solar installations and advanced biofuels. Based in New Delhi, the company is particularly involved in India's efforts to transition to a sustainable energy future.
Key projects and focus areas
  • Pavagada Solar Project: Niche Clean Energy is contributing 603 MW of clean energy to the Pavagada Solar Park in Karnataka, one of the largest solar parks in the world. This project is a key component of India's target of generating 500 GW of renewable energy by 2030.
  • Hemp-based biofuels: The company is developing innovative biofuel technology that uses industrial hemp to produce clean and efficient biomass for ethanol. Hemp is utilized for its high biomass yield and carbon-neutral properties.
  • Technology and efficiency: Niche Clean Energy's strategy involves using advanced technologies, such as modern inverters and AI-driven data analytics, to optimize the efficiency and performance of its solar power plants.
  • Community engagement: The company emphasizes social accountability and works with local communities to ensure its projects benefit the region. This includes creating job opportunities during construction and operation, boosting local economies, and improving infrastructure. 
    •  Environmental and national goals
    Niche Clean Energy's work aligns with several of India's major environmental goals: 
    • Combating climate change: By investing in solar and biofuel projects, the company aims to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and lower India's dependence on fossil fuels.
    • Achieving net-zero emissions: The company's projects are part of the larger national effort to achieve India's goal of net-zero carbon emissions by 2070.
    • Enhancing energy security: Niche Clean Energy contributes to India's energy independence by expanding the country's renewable energy capacity.
