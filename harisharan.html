About Harisharan Devgan

Harisharan Devgan, Chairman of the Niche Group of Companies, is a multi-faceted leader known for his transformative work and diverse interests:
  • Agricultural Innovation: Revolutionized farming through a commitment to sustainable, organic practices that merge traditional and modern methods.
  • Philanthropic Ventures: Actively developing a large Solar Energy park in Karnataka and pioneering cannabis cultivation for affordable community medicines.
  • Cultural Patronage: A passionate film enthusiast who finances and produces Indian cinema.
  • Animal Welfare & Sports: A dedicated animal enthusiast who maintains a gaushala for desi cows and has achieved significant recognition in horse racing.
